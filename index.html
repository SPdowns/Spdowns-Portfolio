    About Me

    I come from San Diego. Actually, I was born in Germany, but was only there for maybe 2 or 3 weeks, my father left the service immediately after. Before coming to Epicodus, I had 3 career paths:

    1. Audio Engineering

      2. In 2003, I started recording music with friends. In 2005, I began recording others professionally. I had a professional grade studio; a live sound package for small groups; and basic musician skills. After two years of barley making money, I switched career paths.

    2. Sketch Comedy Writer

      3. After falling out of love with recording music, I chose to move to Los Angeles and enter the sketch comedy scene. I tried improv and standup, but sketch comedy is what I truly loved and attempted to break into comedy, professionally, through that path. After almost four years of not moving fast enough, I had to return to San Diego.

    3. Retail

      4. After all else failed, retail is where I fell down too. Enjoying people, I thought I could feel content with making a living in that kind of profession. Unfortunately, employees don't make a livable wage, customers aren't considered people, and being treated sub-humanly finally took its toll.

    I chose to learn coding with Epicodus because all the positive changes I've heard occurring in people's lives who were able to attend. Not only are we accumulating programming knowledge, we're also meta learning; learning how to learn if you will. We're learning to work with others and gain soft social working skills. Focusing on the whole professional experience instead of just the facts I feel will lead to more rewarding experiences. And especially excited about Resume and Cover letter training and the mock interviews. If my confidence rises with applying for work, then I know I will have a life that isn't plagued by failing to earn a wage that can support me and a family.
